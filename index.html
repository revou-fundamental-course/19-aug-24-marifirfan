ABOUT MY UNIVERSITY
Universitas Muhammadiyah Malang (UMM) is one of the leading private universities in Indonesia, known for its quality education and dynamic academic environment. Located in the cool city of Malang, UMM offers a variety of study programs focused on the development of science, technology, and Islamic values. With comprehensive facilities, experienced lecturers, and an extensive network of partnerships, UMM is committed to providing a holistic education that encompasses academic, moral, and spiritual aspects. The campus not only focuses on academic achievement but also encourages students to actively participate in extracurricular and social activities, aiming to shape resilient characters who are ready to contribute to society.
One of the standout study programs at UMM is Communication Studies, with a special focus on Public Relations (PR). This program is designed to prepare students to become skilled PR professionals capable of managing communication and public relations strategically. The curriculum covers various aspects of PR, such as media relations, crisis management, digital communication, and PR campaigns, tailored to meet current industry needs. Students are also equipped with practical skills through real-world projects and internships at various companies and organizations. With a learning approach that combines theory and practice, and an emphasis on ethical values and integrity, the program aims to produce graduates who are competent, innovative, and ready to face challenges in the field of communication and public relations.
